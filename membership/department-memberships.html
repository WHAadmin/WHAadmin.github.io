Department Memberships

To extend more scholars greater access to the intellectual life of the WHA, we are now offering departmental memberships.

To extend more scholars greater access to the intellectual life of the WHA, we are now offering departmental memberships.  There are several models listed below, which depend upon your specific institution.  All department members will receive these additional benefits: a further 20% off the membership registration for conferences, 30% off for students from your institution, discounted rates for all departmental membership prices.  In the US & Canada, the primary member will receive publications in paper.  For all other models & for secondary members on this model, publications will be accessible in digital format.

These rates can be found in our online shopping cart beginning November 15, 2021.

  • US & Canada Department Memberships – This first model is for institutions in the US & Canada that are defined as high schools, community colleges and historically Black colleges & universities.
    • Rates: 2 – 4 members $140 annually, 5-7 members $175 annually
  • US & Canada Department Memberships (university only) – This second model is for institutions in the US & Canada that are defined as 4 year universities and do not fit into the category above.
    • Rates: 2 – 4 members $160 annually, 5-7 members $195 annually
  • Overseas Department Memberships – This third model is for institutions overseas that are defined as secondary schools, technical schools & junior colleges.
    • Rates: 2 – 4 members $100 annually, 5-7 members $145 annually
  • Overseas Department Memberships (university only) – This fourth model is for institutions overseas that are defined as 4 year universities.
    • Rates: 2 – 4 members $130 annually, 5-7 members $170 annually
