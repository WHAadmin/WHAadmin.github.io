Support the WHA

There are many ways in which you can support the WHA. First and foremost is your membership in the organization. Consider a multi-year or lifetime membership, and think about a gift membership to a colleague or loved one as well.

The WHA also relies on generous gifts from members and nonmembers alike as an important source of funding, see more on these website pages: Why Give, Endowment, Sponsor & Planned Giving.

Volunteering to help the WHA, whether at conferences, working with affiliates, committees, as an officer, or in many other capacities is also most welcome and critical to the success of this volunteer-run organization. Please contact the WHA with your information and to discuss the best way your talents can be utilized.

You can also spread the word! Let colleagues and friends know how important it is to support the organization which supports them and the field of world history, and that their membership and activity in the WHA are important.

Finally, ensure that your legacy in the field of world history is secured by including the WHA in your planned giving.
