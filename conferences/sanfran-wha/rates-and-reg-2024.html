San Francisco 2024 Rates and Registration

Information on registration rates and how to register for the San Francisco 2024 conference.

Category:

Current WHA members, please Log-in first to receive discount registration rates. If your membership has lapsed, Renew Your Membership before registering for the conference in order to receive the lower member rates.

Although you do not have to be a member in order to register for the conference, if you are not currently a member and would like to take advantage of the discounted member rates, please consider joining the WHA before completing your conference registration: Join the WHA .

To register for the San Francisco 2024 conference, please click here .

CONFERENCE REGISTRATION

Registration to the conference includes:

• Entry to all Conference Panels
• Access to the conference App, online forums, and Hybrid sessions
• Entry to Exhibits
• Attendance at Keynote Addresses and Plenary Sessions
• Opening and Closing Receptions
• Conference Materials
• Morning and Afternoon Beverage Breaks

REGISTRATION DATES

Early Registration runs now through April 1, 2024
Regular Registration runs from April 2 - May 15, 2024
Late Registration begins on May 16, 2024

Lodging

We are finalizing our contract with FSFU for lodging in their residence halls during the conference. We will have more details and a portal to reserve rooms up shortly.

All beds are twin XL. The difference between doubles and singles is the occupant in the room. There will be times where someone requests a single in a bedroom with two beds. The guest suites are junior one bedroom apartments, a little larger than a studio for one person or a couple sharing a unit.

* Rates below are preliminary (for attendees who need to apply for funding), per person, per bed, per night. There will be a one time charge of $30.25 for Linen Services. These prices include full breakfast and lunch.
