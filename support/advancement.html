Supporting The WHA Benefits Teachers & Scholars!

Supporting the WHA benefits teachers & scholars!

Scholars from around the world benefited directly from scholarships to the 32nd Annual WHA Conference, Pittsburgh 2023. They include Dr. Peter Adebayo and Dr. Antonino Crisà (World Scholar Travel Fund).

Additionally, we are extremely grateful to the New England Regional World History Association for providing travel grants to the following recipients: Maximillian Barnett and Professor Nicholas Lepine.

Each November the WHA participates in GivingTuesday, our end-of-year giving campaign. The three areas of focus for 2023 are: Scholarships, the General Fund, and the Endowment. In recognition of this support, all members who give during the period of November 28 through December 22, 2023 will be listed in the World History Bulletin.

The WHA remains thankful to the many members who serve the association with their time and help to make our work possible. In particular, we’d like to thank the Officers and Members of the Executive Council, scholars who have facilitated our “Under the Baobab” series, and members of our various standing committees which oversee different aspects of our shared professional network.

Our mission is to promote activities which raise awareness about world history and engage scholars, teachers, and students in this field of study. To fulfill this duty to our members, we need your support.
